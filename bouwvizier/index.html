bewoner's bouwvizier: web viewer

Try an adapted version of the prototype here, controls are explained below.

place objects in the control window (above) and view them along with their future effects in the window to the right.

1. press the key corresponding with the object (see list).\n2. hover your mouse over your preferred location in the control window.\n3. press space.

you can also select an objects by clicking it in the control window which will let you:\n- move the selected objects with the arrow keys or by right clicking a point on the control window\n- rotate it with the mouse wheel\n- remove it with backspace
