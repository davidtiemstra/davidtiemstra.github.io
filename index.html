ABOUT ME

Hello!
My name is David Tiemstra, I was born in Den Bosch and have been living in Delft for almost five years now, with a short intermezzo in Daejeon, South Korea.

As a designer and as a person I highly value originality, humor & intuition. I think a solution has to not only look right on paper but also feel right, and too tightly defined processes run the risk of marginalizing the unquantifiable.
I can also be very critical, bordering on stubborn, often pushing for design directions that are less popular or overlooked in a team.

I am inspired by digital and physical environments and the way in which the spaces we engage in shape our identity and interactions. Through my work, I would like to support commoning practices and social innovation.

Other interests/inspirations include obscure art, religion, digital subcultures & nostalgia.

As a hobby I like to play around with film & animation, you can view some projects I've done here.
I have also recently been getting better at creative coding and digital prototyping, and you can see some examples here and here.
